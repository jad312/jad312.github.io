About Me

I’m Dr. Jessica Ann Davis, PhD, RN, CCRN, IBCLC (she/her). I am PhD prepared nurse and lactation consultant dedicated to advancing equity in maternal and child health, particularly in the realms of lactation support to enhance long-term health. Here, you'll discover a curated selection of my projects that explore the vital role of breastfeeding for maternal-child health and the unique challenges faced by mothers and families who experience structural barriers and inequities. My work is rooted in creating and implementing community-centered initiatives that empower mothers, enhance their breastfeeding journeys, and provide the support families need during these critical times. By integrating innovative support strategies, I strive to promote health equity and ensure that every family has access to the resources and care they deserve. Whether you’re a parent or family member, fellow researcher, healthcare professional, or someone passionate about improving maternal health, I invite you to explore, collaborate, and contribute to my mission of fostering healthier beginnings for all families. Let’s make a difference together!
