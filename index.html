Optimizing Maternal-Child Health: Research on Lactation and Congenital Heart Defects

Welcome to my GitHub page! I’m Dr. Jess Davis, PhD, RN, CCRN, IBCLC, and I am dedicated to advancing research in maternal and child health, particularly in the realms of lactation support to enhance long-term health. Here, you'll discover a curated selection of my projects that explore the vital role of breastfeeding in supporting infants with congenital heart conditions and the unique challenges faced by mothers and families. My work is rooted in understanding the experiences of families as they navigate the complexities of congenital heart defects. I aim to create community-centered interventions that empower mothers, enhance their breastfeeding journeys, and provide the support families need during these critical times. By integrating innovative telehealth strategies, I strive to promote health equity and ensure that every family has access to the resources and care they deserve. Whether you’re a parent or family member, fellow researcher, healthcare professional, or someone passionate about improving maternal health, I invite you to explore, collaborate, and contribute to our mission of fostering healthier beginnings for all families. Let’s make a difference together!
